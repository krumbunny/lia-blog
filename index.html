Coffee vs Tea

By Lia CP under Coffee Tea

I like coffee and I like tea, so really I have nothing provocative and click bait worthy to say about the subject. What I can say for sure though is that a cup of either one is essential on a cool rainy morning.

My sister only drinks tea. My mom only drinks coffee when it tastes like a candy bar or coffee ice cream. My dad can never make up his mind. My Nana enjoys a pot of straight black coffee every morning.
